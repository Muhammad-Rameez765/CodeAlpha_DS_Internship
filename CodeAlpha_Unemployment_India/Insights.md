**Q2: How did COVID-19 impact unemployment rates?** 
**A:** COVID-19 caused a sharp spike in unemployment, especially during April and May 2020. The national unemployment rate crossed 20%, indicating massive job losses due to lockdowns and business closures.

**Q3: How many people became unemployed during COVID-19?** 
**A:** Based on unemployment rate spikes and employment estimates, tens of millions of people lost jobs during the peak lockdown months, making it one of the worst employment crises in India‚Äôs history.

**Q4: Did unemployment recover after COVID-19?** 
**A:** Yes, unemployment rates gradually declined after lockdown restrictions were lifted. However, the recovery was uneven across states, and some regions continued to experience higher unemployment than pre-COVID levels.
